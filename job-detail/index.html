About the role

Arborlight's Platform team builds the data and orchestration layer that every product surface at Arborlight depends on — queues, internal APIs, and the service mesh that ties it together. You'll own a slice of that platform end to end: design it, ship it, and carry the pager for it.

What you'll do

  • Design and operate distributed services handling 40M+ requests/day across the order and fulfillment pipeline.
  • Lead the migration of two legacy monoliths to event-driven services without downtime.
  • Set technical direction for the platform's data layer, including schema and indexing strategy.
  • Mentor two mid-level engineers and run design reviews for the wider backend guild.
  • Partner with SRE on capacity planning, on-call rotation, and incident postmortems.

Nice to have

  • Experience operating Kafka or an equivalent event stream at production scale.
  • Prior exposure to a regulated domain (fintech, healthtech, or logistics).
  • Open-source contributions to backend or infra tooling.
